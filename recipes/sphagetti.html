Sphagetti

Description

Long, round noodles covered in tomato based sauce and topped with basil. Can also have meat ontop, in the form of ground beef or meatballs

Ingredients

  • Sphagetti Noodles
  • Tomato or Bolognaise sauce
  • OPTIONAL: Ground Beef
  • OPTIONAL: Basil

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles until al dente Meaning, the noodles should be very slightly under done
  2. While noodles are boiling, begin to heat the sauce
  3. Begin to brown the meat, if you decide to use any
  4. After the noodles are cooked, the sauce is hot, and the meat is done you can either serve them mixed together or separetly
