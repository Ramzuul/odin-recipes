Sphagetti

Description

Homemade mac and cheese, a classic comfort food to feed your family

How to Make

A very brief description on how to make this mac and cheese

Ingredients

  • Macaroni Noodles
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Whole Milk
  • Cheddar and Parmesan cheeses
  • Salt, pepper and paprika
  • Bread Crumbs

Steps to make

  1. Boil the noodles, then drain and move to baking dish
  2. Using the butter, flour, and milk make a roux
  3. Using the roux and cheese, make a cheese sauce
  4. Combine the cheese sauce and noodles, then stir until all noodles are coated evenly
  5. Combine bread crumbs, paprika, and parmesan then top the mac and cheese
  6. Bake the mac and cheese until the topping is golden brown, about 30 minutes at 350 degree f
